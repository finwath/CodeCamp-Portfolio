Vladimir Taltos

Steven Brust's Fictional Easterner Assassin from Adrilankha

Artist's rendition of the character with his jhereg familiar, Loiosh.

Some Quick Details about Vlad Taltos:

  • Features in author Steven Brust's 'Dragaera Cycle' series of novels, which currently number at 17 books.
  • Introduced in the book "Jhereg", first published in 1983
  • A facinating protagonist, we are first met with him at the high point of his career as a small-time boss in a criminal organization in the city of Adrilankha.
  • His story arc over the course of the series sees him from his beginning as a tough, wise-cracking, sarcastic but deadly assassin in the back streets of the city to one of the historic figures of Steven Brust's fantasy world that holds the Dragaeran Empire.

For futher information on Steven Brust and his writings, including the bibliography of the 'Taltos Series' you can head over to his official website.
